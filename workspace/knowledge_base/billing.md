# CloudSync Billing FAQ

## How do I upgrade my plan?

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Change Plan**
2. Select your desired plan
3. Review the price difference (prorated for the current billing cycle)
4. Confirm payment

Your new features and limits take effect immediately after payment confirmation.

## What payment methods are accepted?

- Credit/Debit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- PayPal
- Wire transfer (Enterprise plans only, minimum $500)
- Alipay and WeChat Pay (China region)

## How does billing work?

- You are billed monthly on the day you first subscribed
- Invoices are sent to your registered email
- Payment is automatic for credit card and PayPal
- Enterprise annual contracts can be invoiced manually (Net 30)

## Where can I find my invoices?

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Invoice History**
2. All invoices from the past 24 months are available for download (PDF)
3. Click any invoice to download or print

## How do I request a refund?

Our refund policy:

- **Monthly plans**: Refund within 7 days of payment
- **Annual plans**: Refund within 30 days of payment, minus 10% processing fee
- **Usage-based charges**: Not refundable

To request a refund, reply here with your account email and the reason. I will create a ticket for our billing team.

## What happens if my payment fails?

1. You receive an email notification
2. A 7-day grace period begins
3. During the grace period, service continues normally
4. If payment is not resolved within 7 days, your account is downgraded to the Free plan
5. Data beyond the Free plan limits is not deleted but becomes inaccessible until upgrading

## Can I switch from monthly to annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing saves you 20% compared to monthly. To switch:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Switch to Annual**
2. The annual amount is charged at your next billing date
3. Any unused portion of your current monthly period is credited

## Pricing for additional users beyond plan limit?

- Starter plan: $5 per additional user/month
- Professional plan: $8 per additional user/month
- Enterprise: negotiated in contract

Additional API calls: $10 per 10,000 calls beyond your plan limit.
