# CloudSync General FAQ

## How to register an account?

1. Go to https://cloudsync.example.com and click **Sign Up Free**
2. Enter your work email address
3. Set a password (minimum 8 characters, must include upper/lower case letters and numbers)
4. Verify your email by clicking the link we send (valid for 30 minutes)
5. Complete your team profile and start connecting data sources

## How to reset my password?

1. On the login page, click **Forgot Password**
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (valid for 30 minutes)
4. Click the link and set a new password

Note: You can request up to 5 password resets per 24 hours. If you exceed this limit, wait 24 hours or contact support.

## How to delete my account?

Account deletion requires manual processing:

1. Go to **Settings > Account > Delete Account**
2. Confirm by typing your password
3. A support ticket is automatically created
4. Our team will process your request within 3 business days

Before deletion, export any data you want to keep. Deletion is irreversible.
